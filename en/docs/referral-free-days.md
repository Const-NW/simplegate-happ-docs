# How to use SimpleGate for free

SimpleGate has a referral program for users with an active paid subscription.

## How it works

1. Open the account portal.
2. Tap “Invite a friend”.
3. Send your referral link.
4. Your friend can start with a 3-day free trial.
5. After your friend’s first payment, free days are added to your account.
6. If that friend later invites another user, you can receive extra days as well.

## Conditions

- bonus days are added only after the invited user’s first payment;
- no bonus is added for the free trial;
- repeat payments from the same invited user do not add another bonus;
- the program is available to users with an active paid subscription.
