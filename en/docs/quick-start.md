# SimpleGate quick start

## 1. Open the account portal

Open the SimpleGate Telegram bot: https://t.me/SimpleGateVPN_bot

You can activate a 3-day free trial, choose a plan, pay for access and get your personal subscription link.

## 2. Install HAPP

HAPP is the app used to add the SimpleGate connection. It works on mobile, desktop and TV platforms.

## 3. Open the subscription link

After the trial or payment, open the subscription link on the device where HAPP is installed. If the system offers to open it in HAPP, confirm.

## 4. Turn on the connection

Open HAPP and turn on the connection. The system may ask for permission to add a VPN configuration.

## Free use

New users can start with a 3-day free trial. Users with an active paid subscription can use a referral link and receive free days for invitations.
