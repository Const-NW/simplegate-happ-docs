# SimpleGate FAQ

## What is SimpleGate?

A private VPN service for phones, computers and TV devices. Connection is added through HAPP.

## What is HAPP?

A client for proxy/VPN configurations and subscriptions. HAPP itself does not provide servers.

## Is there a trial?

Yes, 3 days free for new users.

## Can I use it for free?

Yes, through the referral program after activating a paid subscription.

## When are free days added?

After the invited user’s first payment. No bonus is added for the trial.
