# What a HAPP subscription link is

A subscription link is a personal link used by HAPP to receive SimpleGate settings. It saves you from entering servers, keys and parameters manually.

## How to add it

HAPP supports adding configurations and subscriptions via URL, QR code, deep link, clipboard and subscription import. In SimpleGate, the user receives a ready personal link that can be opened on a device with HAPP installed.

## Subscription link vs referral link

The subscription link connects your own devices. The referral link invites friends. If a friend pays after using your referral link, free days are added to your account.

## Do not publish your subscription link

It is tied to your access and device limit. For friends, use the referral link.
